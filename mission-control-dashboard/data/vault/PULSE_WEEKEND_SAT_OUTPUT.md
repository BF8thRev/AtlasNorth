👦 SON CHALLENGE — Saturday Morning Edition
**The Sock Toss Tower**
What you need: 5–8 rolled-up socks (ammo), 6–10 plastic cups or empty water bottles (targets).
How to play: Build a tower or pyramid on the floor or a low table. Take turns throwing socks to knock it down — start close, back up each round when you score a hit. Make sound effects. Rebuild and go again.
What skill it builds: Hand-eye coordination, distance judgment, turn-taking, and pure Saturday morning chaos energy.
