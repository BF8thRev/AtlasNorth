# Daily Brief Specification — v1

## Delivery
- Every day at 8:00 AM EST
- Readable in under 5 minutes
- Structured sections only. No commentary outside sections.

## Item 1 — Call Framing (Sales Skill Builder)
**Purpose:** Improve persuasion and shorten explanation time.

Provide:
1. **Framing Shift** — One positioning change that alters how a prospect understands Newton.
2. **Exact Wording** — 1–2 sentences usable verbatim on a call.
3. **What It Overcomes** — The mental resistance it removes (price sensitivity, distrust of software, "we already track this", fear of operator disruption).

Constraints:
- No theory
- No paragraphs
- Usable immediately in a live call

## Item 2 — Re-Engagement Message
**Purpose:** Give a legitimate reason to contact prospects again without pitching.

Provide:
- One short message (5–7 bullets max)
- Framed as an industry observation or operational insight
- **Name the specific prospect from the pipeline this message targets**
- Tailored to one role: COO, extraction lead, or lab manager (rotate daily)

Rules:
- Not a follow-up
- Not "checking in"
- No demo request
- Must naturally invite a response
- **NO fabricated stats.** Every number must be real and sourced.
- **Keep it short.** Tighter than you think.

## Item 3 — Market Signal Post
**Purpose:** Generate attention that turns into conversations.

Provide:
- One LinkedIn/X post
- Based on a real industry trend, behavior pattern, or operational mistake (trending yesterday or today)
- Written in Bryan's voice: analytical, operator-focused, non-promotional

Requirements:
- **Find a REAL trending topic on X or LinkedIn** (cannabis or technology) — Bryan piggybacks it for engagement
- Designed to trigger replies or disagreement
- Not motivational
- Not product marketing
- Under 200 words
- **NO fabricated stats.** If a stat is used, it must be real, sourced, and actionable.
- Include: **Why operators will respond** (one sentence)
- Include: **Source/trend link** when available

## Item 4 — Assistant-Executable Tasks
**Purpose:** Three concrete lowest-hanging-fruit tasks clawbot can complete immediately.

For each task:
- What I will do
- What output I will produce
- What Bryan receives
- **⏱ Time estimate** (how long it takes me to complete)

Goal: Take items off Bryan's plate easily.
