### PAIN POINT 1: C1D1 Camera/Monitor Visibility Gap
**Evidence:**

**Impact:** HIGH | Effort: LOW (we already have C1D1 tablet) | Risk: LOW

**Newton Feature Opportunity:**

- **Efficiency benchmarking** (compare your solvent recovery rate vs industry avg)
- **Leak detection** (flag pressure drops that indicate solvent escape)

**Impact:** VERY HIGH | Effort: MEDIUM | Risk: LOW  
**ROI Pitch:** "Reduce solvent loss by 1% = save $10K-50K/year depending on scale"

## ❌ WHAT'S MISSING FROM NEWTON? (Gaps to Fill)

### 1. No Software-Only Tier Mentioned
**What Community Wants:**
- Data logging apps (hash makers specifically asked for this)
- Spreadsheet alternatives
- Remote monitoring without hardware

**Recommendation:** 
- Offer **Newton Lite** (software-only dashboard for operators with existing sensors/PLCs)
- Price: $200-500/mo, captures smaller operators who can't afford $5K tablet

---

### 2. No Rosin/Solventless Focus (Yet)
**What Community Wants:**
- Ice water hash tracking
- Rosin press yield optimization
- Freeze dryer monitoring

**Recommendation:**
- Newton is currently hydrocarbon/ethanol focused (C1D1 compliance)
- Rosin market is HUGE and growing (solventless = safer = future)
- **Long-term opportunity:** Build Newton for solventless extraction (different sensors, no explosion risk)

---

### 3. No Automation/PLC Integration
**What Community Wants:**
- "I am working on some basic automation... NFPA/NEC codes require special construction for electrical circuits but certain low power devices can be made intrinsically safe with a barrier device" (C1D1 Automation thread, April 2023)

**Recommendation:**
- Operators are DIY-ing automation with Arduino/Raspberry Pi + intrinsically safe barriers
- **Newton could offer:** Pre-built PLC integration kits (plug-and-play automation with Newton as the HMI)

---

### 4. No METRC/Seed-to-Sale Integration
**What Community Wants:**
- "We'll be adding ability for uploading your own spreadsheets/logs soon. I was more referring to having CSVs coming out of the app that are automatically formatted for easy upload to METRC" (July 2023)

**Recommendation:**
- State compliance (METRC, BioTrack, Leaf) is MANDATORY in most legal states
- **Newton should:** Auto-generate METRC-compatible batch reports (huge time saver, huge selling point)

- **Challenge:** $5K tablet may be too expensive for small/budget operators
- **Solution:** Offer software-only tier OR financing/leasing

### 2. DIY Culture
- Lots of threads about building own extractors, automation, etc.
- **Challenge:** DIY operators may try to build their own monitoring (Raspberry Pi + sensors)
- **Solution:** Emphasize C1D1 certification + insurance/compliance benefits (DIY = not compliant)

### 3. Vendor Skepticism
- Precision Extraction went out of business (March 2025)
- ExtractionTek has "multiple issues" (customer complaints in threads)
- **Challenge:** Operators are burned by equipment vendors
- **Solution:** Offer strong warranty, showcase longevity, transparent pricing
