# Bryan's Product: White-Labeled Trainer Certification for HVLP Operators

**Category:** [General] — Product Strategy  
**Date:** 2026-02-14  

---

## Product Overview
- 35-hour live online personal training certification course
- White-labeled for HVLP brands (Crunch, Blink, etc.)
- State-licensed, nationally accredited vocational school
- Key differentiator vs NASM-CPT: live instruction, simplified curriculum, high completion rate
- Trainers can use cert anywhere (portability)
- Designed to turn members into trainers fast

## Core Value Props
1. Solves trainer supply problem (recruit from member base)
2. High completion rate vs traditional cert paths
3. White-label = club-branded experience
4. Portable credential for trainers

## Improvement Opportunities Identified
1. **Member-to-Trainer Pipeline Module** — help operators identify and recruit ideal candidates from existing members
2. **90-Day Post-Cert Mentorship Track** — structured onboarding to reduce new trainer attrition (biggest drop-off point)
3. **Career Path Framework** — Trainer → Senior → Lead → Manager ladder tied to cert levels; creates upsell + retention
4. **Specialty Micro-Certifications** — 5-10 hr add-ons (senior fitness, group, nutrition, sales); recurring revenue
5. **Retention Metrics Dashboard** — completion rate, time-to-first-client, 90-day retention, revenue per trainer; proves ROI to operators
6. **"Trainer-as-Retention-Tool" Positioning** — members who become trainers churn near zero; their clients bond deeper to club

## Revenue Model Expansion
- Base cert: per seat or license fee
- Mentorship: subscription
- Specialties: per course (operator or trainer pays)
- Dashboard: platform fee
- Consulting: pipeline design engagements
