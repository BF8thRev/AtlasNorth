## 3. PAIN POINT ANALYSIS

### Primary Pain Point
**Margin compression under 280E + extraction yield inefficiency at scale.**

Cresco is generating $656M in revenue but still running net losses. They're fighting 280E federal tax treatment (can't deduct normal business expenses), which means every point of extraction yield they lose costs real dollars. At their scale, a 2-3% improvement in extraction yield is worth millions. They expanded PA extraction capacity by 300% — but more throughput at poor efficiency just means more waste at scale.

### Supporting Signals
- Divesting CA: cutting underperforming assets, protecting core margins
- Impairment charges: some assets aren't delivering expected returns
- Gross margin sequential improvement is the current headline — they're laser-focused on cost-per-gram
- QoQ revenue decline despite larger extraction capacity = volume isn't the problem, efficiency/value-per-pound is
