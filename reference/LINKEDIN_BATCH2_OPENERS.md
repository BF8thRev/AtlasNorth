## DELIVERY STRATEGY

**Phase 1: Send Connections (This Week)**
- All 10 connection requests with opener text
- No immediate follow-up message
- Let acceptance sit for 2-3 days

**Phase 2: Follow-up Message (Next Week)**
- After 50%+ acceptance, send short messenger questions
- "How are you thinking about [their specific pain point]?"
- Soft CTA: Share a relevant article or insight

**Phase 3: LinkedIn Warm Bridge**
- Once messenger conversation starts, you have warm context for cold email
- Mention: "Saw your thought on X, figured this might be relevant"
- Creates perceived relationship before real email pitch

---

## Key Differences from Email

✅ **Email:** Value + question + specific insight
✅ **LinkedIn Connection:** Observation + curiosity + light CTA to connect

LinkedIn connection requests work because:
1. Less presumptuous (just adding to network)
2. No inbox pressure (appears in requests, not messages)
3. Sets up warm messenger context later
4. Takes lower effort from their side (one click = accept)
