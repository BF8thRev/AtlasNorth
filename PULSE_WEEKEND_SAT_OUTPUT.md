👦 SON CHALLENGE — Saturday Morning

**🎯 The Sock Ball Sniper**

**What you need:** 3–4 rolled-up sock balls, a laundry basket or cardboard box, a strip of tape for the "throw line"

**How to play:** Set the basket 4–6 feet away. Place the throw line with tape. Each round, move the basket to a different spot — left, right, up on a step, behind a chair. He gets 3 throws per position. Dad tracks "hits" on a whiteboard or piece of paper with tally marks — kids love seeing the score go up.

**Level up:** Add a "moving target" round where you slowly slide the basket while he throws.

**What it builds:** Hand-eye coordination, depth perception, light throwing mechanics, and early scorekeeping/math awareness. The moving basket adds a tracking + anticipation layer that's genuinely developmental at 4.5.

**Setup time: 2 minutes. Chaos level: medium-high. Smiles: guaranteed.**
