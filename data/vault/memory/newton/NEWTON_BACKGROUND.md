## Company Information

Newton Insights is a cannabis extraction operations platform built for extraction teams operating under:
- Tight margins
- Regulatory pressure
- High variability

**Why it was created:**
Teams have data. Teams have SOPs. Teams have experienced staff. Yet yields and outcomes still move in ways no one could explain in real time.

**Who it's for:**
Operators, directors, and executives accountable for results but lacking visibility while execution is happening.

**Scope:** Extraction execution ONLY (not cultivation, retail, or marketing).

---

## What Newton Does

**Core function:**
Makes extraction behavior visible while the batch is running.

**Mechanics:**
- Monitors process behavior
- Highlights when execution drifts from what normally produces stable outcomes
- Shows when changes occur, where they occur, and how long they persist

**What Newton is NOT:**
- Does NOT replace SOPs or operators
- Does NOT require workflow changes
- Does NOT rely on post-run analysis
- Functions as a real-time diagnostic layer on top of existing operations

---

## Why Newton Exists

Most extraction teams learn too late.

**The problem:**
- Yield reports arrive AFTER the batch finishes
- Quality data arrives AFTER product ships
- Spreadsheets explain what happened DAYS LATER
- By the time teams identify a problem, multiple batches are already affected

**Newton's solution:**
Eliminate this delay.

---

## Proof Points (Observed Patterns Across Operators)

Newton consistently surfaces these realities:

1. **Identical inputs still produce different outcomes**
2. **Operator behavior introduces more variation than material**
3. **Small mid-run deviations compound into large yield swings**
4. **Teams overcorrect because they lack attribution**
5. **Senior staff spend time diagnosing instead of preventing**

**Result:**
Newton shortens feedback from **days/weeks to MINUTES**, allowing teams to:
- Detect issues early
- Isolate causes faster
- Stop repeating the same mistakes

---

## How Teams Use Newton

**Operators:**
Use Newton passively — run the process as they always have.

**Managers:**
Monitor stability and identify deviations without relying on anecdotes or memory.

**Executives:**
Gain confidence that execution is under control without waiting for lagging metrics.

**Outcome:**
Newton becomes the shared source of truth across shifts, roles, and facilities.

---

## What Newton Is NOT

- Not a dashboard
- Not a spreadsheet
- Not a consultant
- Not an optimization engine
- Does NOT judge performance
- Does NOT tell teams what to change
- Shows WHAT changed so teams decide

---

## Why Newton Matters

In extraction, **variability is expensive and invisible until it is too late**.

**Teams without real-time visibility:** Forced to guess
**Teams with Newton:** Operate with clarity

**Benefits:**
- Protects margins
- Reduces internal friction
- Restores confidence by making execution observable BEFORE problems become financial losses
