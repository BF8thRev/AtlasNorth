## Why Florida First
Florida MMTCs (Medical Marijuana Treatment Centers) are **vertically integrated by law** — they cultivate, process, AND dispense. Every MMTC runs its own extraction lab. The market is concentrated (~50 licensed MMTCs), targets are identifiable, and the scale of each operation justifies the Newton software investment.

Key Florida MMTCs running extraction operations:
- **Trulieve** — Largest MMTC in Florida. HQ Tallahassee. Massive extraction infrastructure, concentrate product line. Contact: VP Operations / Director of Extraction.
- **Curaleaf Florida** — Major MSO with significant FL extraction capacity.
- **Surterra Wellness** — FL-based, full processing capabilities.
- **Liberty Health Sciences** — FL MMTC, known concentrate products.
- **Columbia Care Florida** — FL MMTC with processing operations.

**Angle:** Florida operators are under margin pressure from competition and can't raise retail prices. Extraction yield improvement = direct bottom-line impact. Newton ROI pitch writes itself.

## Why Michigan Second
Michigan is the **#2 recreational cannabis market by revenue** and has the most licensed cannabis processors of any state (~500+ processor licenses). Hydrocarbon/BHO extraction is common and competitive.

Highest-value Michigan targets:
- **Trucenta** — Self-described as Michigan's largest capacity cannabis oil extraction facility. Based in Michigan. Direct extraction prospect, high volume = high Newton value.
  - Website: trucenta.com
  - Angle: At their volume, even 2-3% yield improvement is significant revenue.
- **Lume Cannabis Co.** — Michigan's largest vertically integrated MSO. Has own processing/extraction.
- **Gage Cannabis Co.** — Major Michigan operator, full vertical integration including extraction.
- **C3 Industries** — Michigan-based, multi-state, heavy concentrates focus.
- **Grassroots Cannabis (Cresco Labs MI)** — Large processor in MI market.
